How CarbonTrack Works

What is a Carbon Account?

A Carbon Account is a tool that tracks CO2 reductions from eco-friendly actions. By understanding our carbon footprint, we can make informed choices for a greener planet.

How to Use CarbonTrack

  • Sign up & log in.
  • Record sustainable actions in the Carbon Accounting Portal.
  • Earn Carbon Credits based on real CO2 reductions.
  • Track progress & join challenges to win rewards!

Why Use a Carbon Account?

  • Quantify your impact on carbon reduction.
  • Encourage long-term eco-friendly habits.
  • Raise awareness & promote energy conservation.
