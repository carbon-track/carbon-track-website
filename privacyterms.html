Privacy Policy

At CarbonTrack, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines what information is collected, how it is used, and what is made public on our platform.

Publicly Visible Information

Only the following information will be visible to other users:

  • Your nickname
  • Your Carbon Credits
  • The school and region you selected in User Center

This limited visibility helps encourage friendly competition and regional participation, while keeping your personal identity protected.

Uploaded Images

To verify your carbon reduction actions, you may choose to upload photos.

  • These images are only reviewed by authorized staff for verification purposes.
  • They will not be publicly shared, published, or used in any announcements without your explicit consent.

Your Privacy, Your Control

We do not collect personal identifiers such as your real name, address, or contact information unless you choose to provide them for communication or rewards delivery purposes.
